# Hooks Library Reference

## 15 Hook Types with Templates & Examples

### 1. CONFESSION HOOK
Reveals something personal/unexpected about the speaker.

**Formula:** "I [unexpected admission]..."
**Psychological trigger:** Authenticity, vulnerability, trust

```
I USED TO FEEL GUILTY ABOUT NOT READING
I SPENT $12,000 ON COURSES BEFORE THIS ONE WORKED
I ALMOST QUIT MY BUSINESS LAST YEAR
I'VE BEEN LYING TO MYSELF ABOUT [X]
I DELETED MY SUCCESSFUL BUSINESS AND STARTED OVER
```

### 2. CONTRARIAN HOOK
Challenges conventional wisdom or common beliefs.

**Formula:** "[Common belief] IS WRONG" or "STOP [accepted practice]"
**Psychological trigger:** Pattern interrupt, curiosity

```
STOP TRYING TO READ MORE BOOKS
YOUR MORNING ROUTINE IS KILLING YOUR PRODUCTIVITY
MOTIVATION IS A LIE. HERE'S WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS
WORKING HARDER WON'T MAKE YOU SUCCESSFUL
THE MORE YOU DIET, THE FATTER YOU GET
```

### 3. QUESTION HOOK
Provocative question that demands an answer.

**Formula:** "[WHY/WHAT IF/ARE YOU] + pain point or desire?"
**Psychological trigger:** Open loop, self-reflection

```
WHY ARE YOU STILL PAYING FOR AUDIOBOOKS?
WHAT IF EVERYTHING YOU KNOW ABOUT [X] IS WRONG?
ARE YOU TIRED OF DIETS THAT STOP WORKING AFTER WEEK TWO?
DO YOU MAKE THESE 5 MISTAKES WITH YOUR MONEY?
WHAT'S STOPPING YOU FROM [DESIRED OUTCOME]?
```

### 4. STATEMENT HOOK
Bold, declarative claim that stops the scroll.

**Formula:** "[Strong claim about reader or world]"
**Psychological trigger:** Authority, certainty

```
YOU'RE NOT LAZY. YOU'RE OVERLOADED.
YOUR SHAMPOO IS THE REASON YOUR HAIR WON'T GROW
2024 WILL BE THE YEAR EVERYTHING CHANGES
THE ECONOMY ISN'T BROKEN. YOUR STRATEGY IS.
YOU ALREADY HAVE EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO SUCCEED
```

### 5. DATA/NUMBER HOOK
Leads with specific number for credibility.

**Formula:** "[Specific number] + [surprising context]"
**Psychological trigger:** Credibility, specificity

```
2,080 HOURS A YEAR IN YOUR CAR
91% OF SAVED ARTICLES ARE NEVER READ
I MADE $47,832 IN 30 DAYS WITH THIS
3 OUT OF 4 BUSINESS OWNERS MISS THIS
$180/YEAR ON AUDIOBOOKS IS INSANE
```

### 6. COMMAND HOOK
Direct instruction to take or stop an action.

**Formula:** "[STOP/START/DON'T] + [action]"
**Psychological trigger:** Authority, urgency

```
STOP PAYING FOR AUDIOBOOKS
DON'T BUY ANOTHER COURSE UNTIL YOU SEE THIS
STOP SCROLLING IF YOU WANT [RESULT]
DELETE YOUR TO-DO LIST. DO THIS INSTEAD.
NEVER SAY THIS TO YOUR KIDS
```

### 7. SOCIAL PROOF HOOK
References others' behavior or results.

**Formula:** "[Number] [people] [action/result]"
**Psychological trigger:** Consensus, FOMO

```
2 MILLION PEOPLE STOPPED READING BOOKS
47,000 CUSTOMERS SWITCHED THIS MONTH
THEY LAUGHED WHEN I [ACTION]. THEN THEY SAW THE RESULTS
WHY EVERY CEO I KNOW USES THIS
THE APP DOCTORS ARE RECOMMENDING
```

### 8. FEAR/WARNING HOOK
Alerts to danger or potential loss.

**Formula:** "WARNING: [threat]" or "YOUR [asset] IS [at risk]"
**Psychological trigger:** Loss aversion, fear

```
YOUR COMPETITORS ARE READING MORE THAN YOU
WARNING: THIS HABIT IS DESTROYING YOUR SLEEP
YOUR BANK IS STEALING $340/MONTH FROM YOU
STOP DOING THIS IMMEDIATELY IF [CONDITION]
YOU'RE LOSING MONEY EVERY DAY WITHOUT KNOWING IT
```

### 9. CURIOSITY GAP HOOK
Creates an information gap that must be closed.

**Formula:** "THE [adjective] [thing] NO ONE TALKS ABOUT"
**Psychological trigger:** Information gap, curiosity

```
THE APP AUDIBLE DOESN'T WANT YOU TO KNOW ABOUT
THE HIDDEN REASON YOUR [X] ISN'T WORKING
THE $5 SECRET THAT REPLACED MY $200 ROUTINE
WHAT HAPPENED WHEN I STOPPED [COMMON HABIT]
THE ONE THING DERMATOLOGISTS WON'T TELL YOU
```

### 10. IDENTITY HOOK
Speaks directly to who the reader is or wants to be.

**Formula:** "FOR [specific identity] WHO [situation]"
**Psychological trigger:** Self-identification, belonging

```
TO PROFESSIONALS OVER 45
THIS IS FOR ANYONE WHO FEELS [EMOTION] RIGHT NOW
IF YOU'RE A [IDENTITY] WHO [STRUGGLE], WATCH THIS
CALLING ALL BUSY MOMS
FOR THE OVERACHIEVER WHO CAN'T SLOW DOWN
```

### 11. TIME-BASED HOOK
Creates urgency through timeframe.

**Formula:** "BY [date], YOU COULD [result]" or "[timeframe] LEFT"
**Psychological trigger:** Urgency, FOMO

```
BY JANUARY 1ST, YOU COULD FINISH 50 BOOKS
IN 7 DAYS THIS DISAPPEARS FOREVER
3 MONTHS AGO I WAS [STRUGGLE]. NOW [RESULT]
WHAT I LEARNED IN 90 DAYS OF [ACTION]
YOUR LAST CHANCE BEFORE [EVENT]
```

### 12. COMPARISON HOOK
Contrasts two options or states.

**Formula:** "[Option A]: [X]. [Option B]: [Y]"
**Psychological trigger:** Anchoring, value perception

```
AUDIBLE: $15/MONTH. THIS APP: $0
250 WORDS/MIN READING. 450 WORDS/MIN LISTENING.
THE OLD WAY VS THE NEW WAY
WORKING 60 HOURS VS WORKING 20 HOURS
BEFORE: 4 HOURS ON REPORTS. AFTER: 15 MINUTES.
```

### 13. RESULT HOOK
Leads with the outcome, not the process.

**Formula:** "[Impressive result] + [qualifier]"
**Psychological trigger:** Desire, possibility

```
52 BOOKS A YEAR (WITHOUT TRYING)
I LOST 47LBS WITHOUT GIVING UP PIZZA
FROM $0 TO $100K/MONTH IN 8 MONTHS
FLUENT IN SPANISH IN 90 DAYS
NEVER MISS ANOTHER WORKOUT
```

### 14. PROBLEM-AGITATE HOOK
Names the problem and twists the knife.

**Formula:** "[Problem statement]. [Why it's worse than they think]."
**Psychological trigger:** Pain amplification

```
THAT STACK OF UNREAD BOOKS ISN'T GOING ANYWHERE
YOUR TO-DO LIST GROWS FASTER THAN YOU CAN CHECK THINGS OFF
EVERY DIET YOU'VE TRIED HAS FAILED. IT'S NOT YOUR FAULT.
YOU'RE WORKING HARDER THAN EVER BUT FALLING FURTHER BEHIND
THE MORE YOU TRY TO SLEEP, THE WORSE IT GETS
```

### 15. PERMISSION HOOK
Gives permission to want or do something.

**Formula:** "IT'S OKAY TO [desire/action]"
**Psychological trigger:** Relief, validation

```
IT'S OKAY TO LEARN DIFFERENTLY
YOU'RE ALLOWED TO WANT MORE
IT'S NOT SELFISH TO PUT YOURSELF FIRST
YOU DON'T HAVE TO HUSTLE 24/7
IT'S OKAY IF TRADITIONAL METHODS DON'T WORK FOR YOU
```

## Fill-in-the-Blank Templates by Category

### Question Templates
```
Are you tired of [pain point]?
What if you could [result] in [timeframe]?
Who else wants [outcome]?
Do you make these [number] [mistakes]?
What's stopping you from [desired outcome]?
Why is nobody talking about [topic]?
What would change if you could [benefit]?
```

### Story Templates
```
I [unexpected action]. Here's what happened…
They [doubted/laughed] when I [action]. But then [result].
A year ago, I couldn't [problem]. Today, I [result].
I accidentally discovered [secret]…
[Timeframe] ago I was [struggle]. Now [impressive result].
The day I stopped [bad behavior] and started [good behavior]…
```

### Benefit Templates
```
Get [result] without [sacrifice].
The fastest path to [outcome] for [audience].
Finally: [result] without [pain point].
How to [benefit] even if [objection].
The [adjective] way to [result] (that actually works).
[Result] in [timeframe] — guaranteed.
```

### Curiosity Templates
```
The [adjective] secret [authority] doesn't want you to know…
The hidden reason why [problem] keeps happening…
The truth about [topic] nobody talks about…
What [successful people] do differently about [topic].
The [number] [thing] that changed everything for me.
```

### Fear/Urgency Templates
```
Warning: [behavior] is destroying your [thing].
Stop [action] immediately if [condition].
Don't [action] until you read this.
[Number] days left before [consequence].
Your [competitor/enemy] is already doing this.
```

### Authority Templates
```
[Notable person/source] reveals [secret] for [result].
[Study/Research] shows [finding] about [topic].
What [experts/top performers] use for [result].
After [impressive credential], I noticed this pattern…
We analyzed [large number] [things]. Here's what we found.
```

### Testimonial Templates
```
"I got [result] in [timeframe]" — [name/type]
Why [number]+ customers switched to [brand]
Real [audience], real results.
See why [impressive number] chose [product].
```

## Power Words for Hooks

### Urgency
Now, Today, Hurry, Limited, Deadline, Last chance, Expires, Act now, Closing, Finally

### Curiosity
Secret, Discover, Reveal, Hidden, Shocking, Mystery, Forbidden, Insider, Behind-the-scenes, Classified

### Fear/Loss
Warning, Danger, Risk, Avoid, Mistake, Lose, Miss, Trap, Destroy, Costly, Never

### Exclusivity
Exclusive, Members-only, VIP, Private, Elite, Premium, Rare, Invitation, Selected, Curated

### Ease
Easy, Simple, Effortless, Instant, Done-for-you, Shortcut, Seamless, Step-by-step, Beginner-friendly

### Transformation
Transform, Unlock, Boost, Skyrocket, Breakthrough, Upgrade, Master, Accelerate, 10x, Level-up

### Trust/Proof
Proven, Tested, Verified, Certified, Authentic, Backed, Evidence, Results, Track record, Guaranteed

### Value
Free, Bonus, Save, Deal, Gift, Reward, Extra, Included, Zero-risk, Unlimited

### First-Word Power Starters
You, How, Why, Stop, Warning, New, Free, Finally, Secret, Introducing, Imagine, What if

## Hook Scoring Checklist

Before finalizing any hook, verify:

- [ ] Stops scroll in 0.5 seconds?
- [ ] Evokes emotion (curiosity, fear, desire)?
- [ ] Reader recognizes themselves?
- [ ] Creates open loop?
- [ ] Specific enough (not generic)?
- [ ] 10 words or fewer?
- [ ] No clichés?
- [ ] Works without supporting text?
- [ ] Matches awareness level?
- [ ] Has implied payoff (not empty clickbait)?
