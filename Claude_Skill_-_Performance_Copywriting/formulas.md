# Copywriting Formulas Reference

## Core Formulas (Use These Most)

### 1. PAS — Problem, Agitate, Solution
**Best for:** Short-form, Meta ads, email subjects, pain-driven markets

| Step | Function | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Problem** | Identify specific pain | "Tired of waking up exhausted even after 8 hours?" |
| **Agitate** | Twist the knife | "That brain fog follows you all day. Coffee doesn't help anymore. You're snapping at people you love." |
| **Solution** | Present relief | "This sleep supplement fixed my broken sleep in 3 nights." |

**Variations:**
- PASO (+Outcome): Adds visualization of result after solution
- PASOP (+Outcome+Proof): Adds social proof after outcome
- PADS (+Differentiation): Explains why this solution differs

---

### 2. BAB — Before, After, Bridge
**Best for:** Transformation products, weight loss, self-improvement

| Step | Function | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Before** | Current painful state | "Before: Spending 4 hours on reports every week" |
| **After** | Desired future state | "After: Reports done in 15 minutes, automatically" |
| **Bridge** | Your product connects them | "Here's how our tool makes it happen..." |

---

### 3. AIDA — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action
**Best for:** Universal — ads, emails, landing pages, sales letters

| Step | Function | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Attention** | Stop the scroll | "I deleted my $50K business and started over" |
| **Interest** | Build curiosity | "Here's what I learned rebuilding from zero..." |
| **Desire** | Create want | "Now I work 4 hours a day and make more than ever" |
| **Action** | Direct CTA | "Want the exact playbook? Link in bio" |

**Variations:**
- AIDCA (+Conviction): Adds proof layer before action
- IDCA: For warm audiences (skip attention)
- AIDPPC: Attention → Interest → Description → Persuasion → Proof → Close

---

### 4. PASTOR — Problem, Amplify, Story/Solution, Transformation, Offer, Response
**Best for:** Long-form sales letters, webinar scripts, launch emails

| Step | Function | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Problem** | Identify pain | "I know how frustrating it is when..." |
| **Amplify** | Make it worse | "And it's only getting worse because..." |
| **Story** | Relatable narrative | "I was in the same place when..." |
| **Transformation** | Show change | "Here's what happened for Sarah..." |
| **Offer** | Present solution | "That's why I created..." |
| **Response** | Clear CTA | "Click below to start your transformation" |

---

### 5. Hook-Story-Offer (Russell Brunson)
**Best for:** Funnels, webinars, social media, video scripts

| Step | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Hook** | Capture attention immediately |
| **Story** | Build connection and desire through narrative |
| **Offer** | Present the irresistible offer |

---

## Additional Formulas

### 6. FAB — Features, Advantages, Benefits
**Best for:** Product descriptions, tech products, spec-heavy items

| Level | Question | Example (Project Tool) |
|-------|----------|------------------------|
| **Feature** | What is it? | "Real-time collaboration" |
| **Advantage** | So what? | "Everyone sees changes instantly" |
| **Benefit** | Why care? | "No more 'which version is latest?' chaos" |

---

### 7. 4Ps — Picture, Promise, Prove, Push
**Best for:** Landing pages, sales pages, direct mail

| Step | Function | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Picture** | Vivid outcome | "Imagine checking your stats and seeing 40%, 45%, 50%..." |
| **Promise** | Clear claim | "Double your email open rates in 7 days" |
| **Prove** | Evidence | "Here's how we did it for 500+ businesses..." |
| **Push** | Urgency CTA | "But this pricing ends Friday" |

---

### 8. 4Us — Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific
**Best for:** Headlines, hooks checklist

Score each 1-4. **12+ tends to outperform.**

| U | Question |
|---|----------|
| **Useful** | Does it help them? |
| **Urgent** | Why now? |
| **Unique** | Why you? |
| **Ultra-specific** | For who exactly? |

Example: "The 3-minute morning routine busy moms use to lose 10lbs by summer"
- Useful: weight loss ✓
- Urgent: by summer ✓
- Unique: 3-minute + for busy moms ✓
- Ultra-specific: moms, 10lbs ✓

---

### 9. ACCA — Awareness, Comprehension, Conviction, Action
**Best for:** Complex products, B2B, educational content

| Step | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Awareness** | Make aware of problem/opportunity |
| **Comprehension** | Help understand solution |
| **Conviction** | Build belief with proof |
| **Action** | Tell exactly what to do |

---

### 10. QUEST — Qualify, Understand, Educate, Stimulate, Transition
**Best for:** Lead gen, educational marketing, expert positioning

| Step | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Qualify** | Identify ideal reader immediately |
| **Understand** | Show you get their situation |
| **Educate** | Teach something valuable |
| **Stimulate** | Create excitement |
| **Transition** | Smooth move to next step |

---

### 11. Star-Chain-Hook
**Best for:** Ads, short sales copy, testimonial content

| Step | Function | Example |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Star** | Big idea/selling point | "She went from $0 to $100K/month in 8 months" |
| **Chain** | Connected reasons | "First, she fixed her offer. Then her targeting. Then her funnel." |
| **Hook** | CTA with incentive | "Get the exact framework she used (free guide)" |

---

### 12. SCQA — Situation, Complication, Question, Answer
**Best for:** B2B content, consulting, proposals, executive communication

| Step | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Situation** | Current context |
| **Complication** | Problem or challenge |
| **Question** | Key question this creates |
| **Answer** | Your solution |

---

### 13. The Hero's Journey (Simplified for Ads)
**Best for:** Video scripts, brand stories, transformation narratives

| Stage | Ad Application |
|-------|----------------|
| **Ordinary World** | "I was a stressed accountant, 40lbs overweight..." |
| **Call to Adventure** | "Then my doctor said 3 words that changed everything..." |
| **Trials & Failures** | "I tried every diet. Nothing worked long-term." |
| **Discovery** | "Until I discovered this one simple change..." |
| **Transformation** | "6 months later, I ran my first marathon" |
| **Return & Share** | "Now I'm sharing exactly what I did" |

---

### 14. Monroe's Motivated Sequence
**Best for:** Presentations, pitches, video scripts

| Step | Function |
|------|----------|
| **Attention** | Hook |
| **Need** | Establish problem |
| **Satisfaction** | Present solution |
| **Visualization** | See life with solution |
| **Action** | Exactly what to do |

---

### 15. Bob Stone's 7-Step Direct Mail Formula
**Best for:** Direct mail, sales letters, long-form landing pages

1. Promise biggest benefit in headline
2. Immediately expand on that benefit
3. Tell exactly what they'll get
4. Back up with proof and testimonials
5. Tell them what they'll lose if they don't act
6. Recap key benefits
7. Incite action now

---

### 16. StoryBrand SB7 Framework
**Best for:** Brand messaging, websites, overall marketing strategy

| Element | Key Insight |
|---------|-------------|
| **Character** | Customer is hero (NOT your brand) |
| **Problem** | External, internal, philosophical |
| **Guide** | Your brand (empathy + authority) |
| **Plan** | Simple 3-step plan |
| **CTA** | Direct and transitional |
| **Failure** | What they lose if no action |
| **Success** | Life when they succeed |

---

### 17. SUCCES (Made to Stick)
**Best for:** Creating memorable messages, content that sticks

- **Simple:** Find the core message
- **Unexpected:** Break patterns
- **Concrete:** Specific, sensory language
- **Credible:** Believable details/proof
- **Emotional:** Make them feel
- **Stories:** Drive action through narrative

---

## Quick Formula Selection Guide

| Situation | Best Formula |
|-----------|--------------|
| Meta ad (short) | PAS, BAB, AIDA |
| Meta ad with story | Hook-Story-Offer |
| Landing page | 4Ps, Bob Stone's 7-Step |
| Sales letter | PASTOR, Perry Belcher 21-Step |
| Email subject | PAS, Curiosity gap |
| B2B/Complex product | ACCA, SCQA |
| Video script | Hero's Journey, Monroe's Sequence |
| Testimonial ad | Star-Chain-Hook, SSS |
| Brand messaging | StoryBrand SB7 |

---

## Formula Application by Awareness Level

| Awareness | Best Formulas | Focus |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| **Unaware** | Hero's Journey, Story hooks | Don't mention product, reveal problem |
| **Problem Aware** | PAS, BAB | Agitate pain, introduce solution category |
| **Solution Aware** | FAB, 4Ps | Differentiation, mechanism, why you |
| **Product Aware** | AIDCA, Testimonial | Proof, objection handling |
| **Most Aware** | Direct offer, CTA-focused | Urgency, easy action, deal |
